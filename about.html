About Me

I am Vijay

I am a professional actor with over 10 years of experience in the film and theater industry. Born and raised in Chennai, I have always had a passion for storytelling and performance arts.

My journey began in local theater groups where I honed my skills in voice modulation, expression, and stage presence. Over the years, I have transitioned to the big screen, starring in numerous commercially successful and critically acclaimed films.
